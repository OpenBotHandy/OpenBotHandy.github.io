About OpenBotHandy

OpenBotHandy is an open source project to develop robot manipulation technology. OpenBotHandy provides manipulation benchmark scenarios, real-time simulation, and baseline manipulation codes, which enable researchers and developers to experiment with new ideas with Samsung BotHandy and advance robot manipulation technologies.

Manipulation Benchmark

"Clearing a Table"

The objective of "Clearing a Table" to evaluate manipulation skill for cleaning up after a meal.

Manipulator - Franka Emika Panda

Gripper - Samsung BotHandy Gripper

The task requires the robot to recognize objects (plate, bowl, cup, wine glass, and utensils) on a table and place them in the desired zone in the tray after removing food leftovers into a trash bin. We provide 15 benchmark test scenarios with diverse arrangements of kitchenware and conditions. The detailed description is in the rule book.
